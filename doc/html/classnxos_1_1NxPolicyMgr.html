Detailed Description

Policy Mgr Class to hold information about a list of various Packet filtering policies. A Policy looks like ex) plc_name: P1 seq_id: 1 match1: match2: Action1: Action2: seq_id: 2 match1: match2: Action1: Action2:

  • packets destined to the switch (punted to the s/w)
  • MACs Discovered, moved, Deleted
